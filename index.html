Aims

Brush and Pastey is a free, award winning oral health app helping to reduce and prevent childhood tooth decay.

Made for young children and developed by Dentists, the app is designed to motivate and educate children through instructional, rewarding gameplay.

Based on science and made with a friendly design, the app transforms brushing into a game for children and offers parents the advice they need to maintain child dental health
